## Summary

BoardTAU is a web-based platform designed to facilitate accommodation search and management for the Tarlac Agricultural University community. The project involves interaction with human participants and collection of personal data, with appropriate measures in place to address security and privacy concerns. The study does not involve vulnerable populations, physical/psychological risks, animal experimentation, or environmental hazards. Informed consent is obtained through user registration agreements. The research primarily focuses on software development, testing, and evaluation with human users.
